Welcome to DiabEye

DiabEye is a powerful tool designed to assist in diagnosing Diabetic Retinopathy (DR)

Our model analyzes medical images and predicts the stage of DR to assist healthcare professionals in making informed decisions

Diabetic retinopathy is a diabetes-related complication that affects the eyes, particularly the retina.
This condition occurs when high blood sugar levels damage the tiny blood vessels in the retina, the light-sensitive tissue at the back of the eye.

Use our "Predict" tool to analyze patient data and predict the DR stage based on the submitted image.
